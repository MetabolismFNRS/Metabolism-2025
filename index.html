FNRS Metabolism Contact Group

Second Meeting

We are pleased to announce the second meeting of the FNRS Metabolism Contact Group, established within Le Fonds de la Recherche Scientifique (FNRS), the National Fund for Scientific Research in the French-speaking community of Belgium.

As we continue to push the boundaries of knowledge in metabolism research, this meeting marks an important step forward in our shared mission to decipher the complex biochemical processes that sustain life.

Metabolism lies at the heart of biological systems, influencing everything from cellular function to organismal health. The scope of our community spans basic and translational research, including disease mechanisms, energy homeostasis, and metabolic regulation.

The FNRS Metabolism Contact Group brings together researchers, clinicians, and industry stakeholders with a common goal: to advance scientific understanding through collaboration and knowledge exchange. By fostering interdisciplinary dialogue and building new partnerships, we aim to accelerate innovation and translate discoveries into real-world impact.

This second meeting offers a vibrant platform to present recent findings, engage in scientific discussions, and develop collaborative networks. We will highlight emerging themes, confront current challenges, and shape the research agenda for years to come.

We warmly welcome researchers at all career stages—established scientists, early-career investigators, and students alike. Your participation is vital to building a strong and inclusive metabolism research community in Belgium and beyond.

We look forward to welcoming you to the second meeting of the FNRS Metabolism Contact Group.
